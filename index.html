There once was a heart set aflame,
Every beat softly whispered your name.
Through joy and through time,
One question feels prime:
So please… will you be my valentine?
There once was a love tried by time, Not fair as the stars, yet sublime. Though summers may fade, And roses feel staged, No glow outshines yours, so uniquely mine. Yes, Your Highness, your quiet elegance gleams anywhere, And your laughter makes light everywhere. If beauty be true, It lives just in you— In moments so honest and rare; I love you more than I know how to share.
